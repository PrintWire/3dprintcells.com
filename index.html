Bioprinting Services

01

Custom Bioink Formulation

Gelatin methacrylate (GelMA), alginate, fibrin, and decellularized ECM bioinks formulated to your cell type and application. Rheology tuned for printability while preserving growth factor binding and matrix stiffness.

02

Cell-Laden Construct Printing

Extrusion bioprinting of cell-laden hydrogels in anatomically derived geometries. Dual-material builds with sacrificial channels for vascularization. Constructs seeded with primary cells, iPSCs, or immortalized lines per protocol.

03

Assay-Ready Tissue Models

High-throughput bioprinted tissue models for drug toxicity screening and disease modeling. 96-well format compatibility. Standardized barrier function, contraction, or metabolic output metrics supplied with each batch.

BIOINK LIBRARY

Bioink Material Platform

The bioink defines the construct. Our library covers the full range from soft neural hydrogels to stiff cartilage matrices — each formulated for printability and biological performance.

  • ● GelMA — photocrosslinkable, tunable stiffness 1–50 kPa
  • ● Alginate — rapid ionic crosslinking for fast-set constructs
  • ● Fibrin — native wound-healing matrix for vascular models
  • ● Decellularized ECM — tissue-specific biological cues preserved
  • ● PEGDA — biorthogonal chemistry for click-crosslinking
  • ● Composite bioinks combining structural and biological signals
